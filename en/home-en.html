As an independent artist from Berlin, Doris Menzel deals with topics such as oppression, abuse, isolation and pain. Her works reveal the core of interpersonal structures and human relationships. They position the viewer to dive deeper into perception of societal dissonance, as well as misery of the indviduals.
‍
Doris Menzel studied at the Berlin University of the Arts, graduated with a diploma under the apprenticeship of Vivienne Westwood. Throughout her live she took on various management positions in the clothing industry, working for a number of international fashion brands.
‍
She came to freelance work through her sustainable label “Your Local Dealer”, which is known for artfully designed clothing pieces, using screen printing and other sustainable manufacturing processes.
